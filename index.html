Not everyone on your team processes information the same way. AutoFlow ships with three persona-based templates — each one cascades from admin configuration down to how each rep sees their day.

01 — Template
The Analyst
Numbers-first. Process-obsessed.

Won't make a move without data behind it. Needs pipeline metrics and lead scoring visible before picking up the phone.

  • Lead score always front and center
  • Close rate and source performance
  • Rep-level activity tracking
  • Funnel stage conversion view
  • Response time monitoring
02 — Template
The Visualizer
Board-thinker. Pattern-reader.

Needs to see the whole floor before working it. Color, layout, and spatial flow communicate more than a list.

  • Kanban pipeline by stage
  • Customer journey timeline per lead
  • Color-coded status and urgency
  • Live floor overview
  • Visual appointment calendar
03 — Template
The Driver
Action-first. Floor-native.

Doesn't want a dashboard — wants to know who to call right now and how to log it fast. Built for closers.

  • Priority queue — who needs attention now
  • One-tap activity logging
  • Fast follow-up assignment
  • FAB-driven mobile experience
  • Minimal UI, zero friction
